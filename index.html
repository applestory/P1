WHO "I" AM

Gosse International Student Residence has been providing outstanding, affordable student accommodation since 1997 for local and international students in Adelaide, South Australia.Conveniently located in the beautiful, sought after leafy suburb of Fullarton, just south of the Adelaide CBD, close to public transport with easy access to the city and Adelaide Uni, Uni SA, CALUSA, SAIBT and Tafe.We offer a unique living environment that goes beyond the scope of other student accommodations. Modern amenities and energetic staff who do everything possible to provide a secure, supportive home during your time in Adelaide.Our experienced managers live on site and provide an unobtrusive presence available to help with every need from routine maintenance and repair to emotional support in times of stress or joy. We invite you to visit us for a tour.

Facilities

Single or shared fully furnished rooms, communal kitchens, lounge, recreational and TV rooms, outdoor BBQ area, computer rooms, gender specific bathrooms, laundries and amenities are all included in the price.

Rates

Our student accommodation is cost effective and all inclusive. Features include wireless internet and fully furnished accommodation with bedding supplied and laundered weekly. We support two types of room, which are Single Room ($165/w) and Twin Shared Room ($130/p/w), and we also provide Casual Rent ($35/p/n). The Bond is $320 for Single and Twin Rooms.

FAQ

Is internet available and is it included in the rent?

The residence supplies free unlimited use of fast wireless internet to tenants, with unlimited data for all to use and enjoy!You can connect to your own personal internet provider using the telephone line in your room if you for whatever reason prefer your own connection.

Do you pay more for electricity and water?

The rent covers all charges for electricity, gas and water and also includes a weekly bed linen change.

How far away is the residence away from the city?

We are located approximately 4 km south from the city centre. Most students travel daily by bus, which arrive every 15 minutes from our closest bus stop which is only a 2 minute walk away. Some students purchase bicycles and cycle daily to uni, we have bike racks available at the residence.

Do I need to buy anything for my room?

All rooms are fully furnished and air-conditioned (heating and cooling). We provide linen and cutlery/crockery, but you will need to purchase your own pot/cooking pan. You are more then welcome to bring or purchase your own items or furniture. Staff are more then happy to help you rearrange your room the way you'd like it for the duration of your stay.
